About

Like Startup Weekend Honolulu and City Camp, this competition will focus on pitching ideas, forming teams, and building a software application in under 50 hours. The entries will be judged by software industry professionals, and prizes will be awarded.

Get Ready

We will be hosting weekly workshops on Tuesday and Thursday of each week (see schedule below) leading up to the start of the hackathon (May 13th). The topic of each workshop may vary from technology tutorials, to structured presentations from select speakers.

For questions like "How do I participate?" see the FAQ.
